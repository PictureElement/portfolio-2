About me

I'm Marios Sofokleous. I'm currently based in Pafos, Cyprus, an island country in the Eastern Mediterranean. I have a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Cyprus.

I enjoy writing about IT-related staff, and I have a few unpublished books that are quite extensible. I love coding and design that's why I decided to follow a career in Web development. Since my graduation in 2016, I’ve been learning various important skills and tools to help me excel in this sector, such as the Linux system, Ethical hacking, and Git/GitHub. I’ve started Front-end fundamentals on January 2017 and since then I’ve been building a website dedicated to front-end technologies.

I love the feeling of sitting countless hours in front of a computer screen, thinking ways to tackle a problem. I think It's fun and rewarding experience. I don't give up easily and my strong will and tenacity help me overcome challenges and find any solution. I don't like ambiguities and I try to clear things up before dealing with any problem. I have to admit that I am a perfectionist and I spend a great deal of time perfecting my "creations". I can't settle on basic explanations, I always dig deeper in order to gain a more profound understanding. I am always eager to learn new technologies and tools and I am flexible enough to work in any IT-related field.
